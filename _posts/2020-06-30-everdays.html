Everdays is a mobile and web app that connects the families and loved ones of someone who has passed away. My time working with Everdays was spent as the startup's primary UI designer.

Alongside executive and business leaders, marketing and creative, and the dedicated developers for each platform, I designed and helped implement UIs for native iOS and Android apps, web apps (consumer and b2b), as well as developing a visual language, styleguide, and icon set to be used across all platforms. I took part in whiteboarding sessions and design work including sketches, wireframes, prototypes, and full-fidelity mockups.
